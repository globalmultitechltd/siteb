Privacy Policy for Spirit Communicator

Last Updated: October 08, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Spirit Communicator. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our Android application.

2. Information We Collect

Spirit Communicator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data.

3. Permissions We Use

To provide the best experience, our app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera
    • Usage: To control the device's flashlight for visual feedback.
    • Required: No
  • Location
    • Usage: For enhanced functionality related to sensor data.
    • Required: Yes, for accessing fine and coarse location.
  • Vibrate
    • Usage: To provide haptic feedback during interactions.
  • Foreground Service
    • Usage: To keep the app running in the background for continuous sensor monitoring.
  • Wake Lock
    • Usage: To keep the device screen on during active use.
  • Battery Optimization Exemption
    • Usage: To prevent the app from being restricted by battery optimization settings.

4. How We Use Permissions

  • Camera: Activates the flashlight for event-driven visual cues.
  • Location: Enhances sensor-based functionalities.
  • Vibrate: Provides tactile feedback to users.
  • Foreground Service & Wake Lock: Ensures the app runs smoothly in the background without interruptions.
  • Battery Optimization Exemption: Maintains uninterrupted app performance.

5. Data Sharing

Spirit Communicator does not share your information with third parties.

6. Security

We prioritize your security and implement reasonable measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
